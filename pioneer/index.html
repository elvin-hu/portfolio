Resumé
“I hate this typeface”

TIME

2018

SUMMARY

Design with the typeface you hate the most

CATEGORY

Graphic Design

Project introduction

1. Choose one typeface that you think is terrible/awful/bad/ugly/etc. or, in other words,
one you HATE from those displayed in class.

2. Create 12 iterations of type compositions
with the chosen typeface.

3. Create 2 final design with the typeface, after
figuring out ways to make it "look better" and
compensate its "shortcomings"

Choosing a typeface

I chose ITC Pioneer out of the 30+ options.

Sketches

I chose this typeface because personally I was not a big fan of the faux-3D cast shadow and the blockniness. To challenge myself, however, I want to see whether I can turn these two qualities into things that I actually like. Here are two of the many digital sketches I made initially.

After making the sketches, I found a few ways to make this typeface appeal to me: stack words vertically, embrace the shadow to create 3D effect and exaggerate the scale so that the form becomes abstract shapes.

Final directions

Well, as I mentioned, I don't like this typeface, so to make my life easier, I decided to work with the titles of the album and the tour of my favorite singer's.

I found out that the U and H forms a really interesting shape together, and the "blockiness" works well under a sci-fi theme.

Final design

Reflection

I know this project has a title, "I hate this typeface", but surprisingly, I actually kind of like it now?

In my design career it is impossible that I will always be working on things I like or I am passionate about. Being able to embrace what I don't like and see the good things in everthing is an essential skill to have.
